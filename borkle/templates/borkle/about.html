Welcome to Borkle!

Borkle is a dice game where you try to roll scoring combinations to get the most points. You know, it's a lot like that game Farkle.

The Rules

  • Roll the dice to score points!
  • There is no minimum score needed to get on the board
  • You can keep rolling as long as you keep scoring
  • For each roll, you can select any scoring dice combination to table it. This removes the dice from play
  • If you have tabled all six of your dice, you can start fresh with all six dice back in play and keep building on your score!
  • If you roll and don't have any possible score, your turn is over and you lose your points for that turn
  • Once some one reaches the game's max score, each other player gets one more turn to try to beat the highest scoring player and win
  • At the end of the game, the player with the highest score wins!
  • There is no tie breaking mechanism, so games can result in ties
  • Refer to the score chart to see possible dice scores
